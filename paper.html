Presentacio de Paper

"If at first the idea is not absurd, then there is no hope for it."

Well, Impulse provides a huge platform to shoot your technical stuffs. Highlight your enrichment loud enough, and master everyone to stand amidst the prevailing technological development. “Innovation distinguishes between a leader and a follower”. Be a leader and let your thesis speak...

Rules

Topic: Any mechanical stream

  • The participants should submit an abstract of their research on or before September 5.

  • The papers will be selected based on clearly defined objective, rationale for study.

  • Maximum of two authors.

  • The selected participants would receive a confirmation mail from Impulse team on September 7 and they must mail the entire thesis to impulsem2k15@gmail.com.

  • Thesis must be strictly in IEEE format.

  • Presentation shall be strictly in MS-OFFICE PowerPoint (maximum of 15 -16 slides) and should be in landscape form.

  • Presentation time of maximum 8 minutes shall be allotted followed by a 2 minute query session.

  • Participants will be judged on the following criteria:

    • Oration skill

    • Quality of presentation

    • Appropriate use of statistics

    • Originality of research

    • Content of research

    • Ethical considerations

    • Response to queries

Prizes

  • Winner will be awarded with “Best Paper Award” along with a cash prize of 1500 INR.

  • Participants will be awarded with participation certificates.
